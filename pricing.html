Dr. Cellucci's Facial Skincare Recommendations

Basics
  1. Have a Vi Peel applied to your skin.
  2. After two weeks, apply Obaji products daily for four months.
  3. Discontinue all treatments and strictly avoid the sun for 1-2 months.
  4. Repeat.
Details

My personal recommendation for ongoing facial skin care begins with the application of our Vi Peel. This step goes a long way in showing us what your natural skin is under all the debris and superficial discoloration that is present. There is no preparation needed for the Vi Peel except no sun exposure at least one week prior and no use of Retin-A for at least 2 weeks prior.

After about two weeks, begin using Obaji skin products regularly as recommended. After about four months of using these products, allow your skin to rest and do nothing except cleanse, moisturize and apply sun block for about 1-2 months. This will allow your native skin and colors to emerge, giving you a new baseline. From that point, repeat the entire process, beginning with the Vi Peel.

There are many additions that can be made while going through the above steps with your face. At any time, we can inject Botox and/or filler, depending on the look you desire.

Laser Hair Removal — $50-250

$50 per small area (ex: face, bikini, armpits)
$200 per large area (ex: legs, back)
Special: $250 for legs, bikini and armpits

Medi spas are often sought out for laser hair removal, the number one selling service we provide. Due to technological advances in recent years, laser hair removal has become an art of precision facilitated by a machine called the Vectus. It uses a skintel device that will measure an individual's exact melanin index in order to ensure the most precise treatments available. This number varies not only between individuals, but also amongst different body parts. Your melanin index will be checked before every treatment to ensure that results from our hair removal system are both impressive and delightful.

As average persons require 4-8 treatments, we recommend scheduling between periods of 6-8 weeks. It is essential that you only shave before and in between treatments, as any other method of hair removal (plucking, tweezing, waxing, depilatories, etc) will result in less than ideal results. Please be sure to shave the areas to be treated shortly before your treatment session (within 24 hours is most ideal). Another crucial reminder is that the treated areas cannot be exposed to sunlight, two weeks before and after each treatment. Areas that are exposed to the sun, (preferably in small amounts) can be covered with clothing and/or sunblock to reduce risk of burning. We gladly remove hair from any part of the body including private areas, and treat all skin types, from very light to dark. Remember that darker hair is the most receptive to laser treatment.

Botox — $9 per unit

A derivative of botulinum toxin, Botox functions as a muscle paralytic by blocking chemical signals from the nerves. Injecting small amounts into the muscles is a treatment widely in practice (3.2 million procedures a year) to smooth wrinkles in between and around the eyes, as well as the forehead. Botox generally lasts around 3-4 months, and is recommended to be re-injected before the return of muscle function and keep those wrinkles at bay.

Fillers — Juvederm, Radiesse, Belotero and Voluma — starting at $400/500

$400 per syringe

Each of these fillers come with their own respective benefits depending on the facial sculpting you desire. Juvederm is a smooth consistency gel comprised of naturally occurring hyaluronic acid, one of the few acids that is FDA approved for use as a facial filler. It is excellent for hydrating skin and adding volume to applied areas. Dermal fillers like Radiesse work by boosting collagen into injected areas, and treating nasolabial folds. They generally last around 9 months, and Voluma, one of the newest fillers, lasts up to two years in application of sculpted cheekbones.

VI Peel — $200

Originally developed to treat acne, this physician-grade chemical peel provides a virtually painless treatment for almost all skin types and conditions. Any age can benefit from 2-4 treatments a year to reverse sun damage and hyperpigmentation. It’s a well-established and highly productive chemical peel that works synergistically and can be applied in a matter of moments. It generally causes the skin to take on a red/orange tinge for the first two days, which then leads to peeling of the skin for days 3-5. This peeling can be concealed well with moisturizer and makeup. Feel free to cover it if you feel uncomfortable in public places during the days that you are peeling. Medically you are stable to resume normal activities immediately after application with the definite exception of sun exposure.

Asclera Vein Therapy — $100 per 2ml vial

Asclera yields excellent and immediate results on unsightly veins on the legs. It is injected directly into the veins causing micro scarring and permanent termination of blood flow through that vein. It causes a burning sensation at the injection site which resolves within seconds. After injection, you are encouraged to walk for 3-5 minutes then to wear tight hose stockings for 2-3 days for best results. Generally one vial with 2ml of product will treat several small veins or 3-4 larger veins. We are happy to evaluate your leg veins and give you an idea of how much product will give you the best result. This product can be used on varicose veins and has yielded excellent results.

Obagi — varies based on product

Obagi was launched out of Long Beach, CA in 1988, and ever since has been applied for revitalizing, enhancing, and maintaining skin. Latisse — $200

A synthetic form of the naturally occurring substance prostaglandin, Latisse is an excellent and well-founded product. It is renowned for its use in treatment of hypotrichosis, wherein normal eyelash growth fails to occur. By applying a small drop of Latisse to a small brush and swiping it across your upper eyelid daily, it causes upper eyelashes to be longer, thicker, and significantly more full. Many people have had excellent results with this. Keep in mind that side effects are by no means permanent and that normal growth resumes upon termination of application. Take care to avoid dropping in your eye or using with herbal products.

Younique Mascara — $40 per set

After applying a thin layer of gel, you apply small fibers to the gel causing your eyelashes to be full, long and gorgeous. Typically,your lashes will become up to three times larger. Then the gel is reapplied over the fibers without your lashes feeling crumbly or crunchy. This product also does an excellent job of separating each lash and enhancing the natural aesthetics of your eyes.
