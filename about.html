About Us

About Jeuvani

Jeuvani will be a state-of-the art medi-spa offering a variety of services and products. Previously known as Bella TAZA, which is located in Davis and will be closing its doors on December 22, 2014, Jeuvani will be opening a new rejuvenation center in Folsom in January, 2015. Our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and community has been the same for many years, and is what continues to drive our business. Beginning in January, we look forward to servicing clients living in Folsom, as well as others traveling to the area from out of town. Our spa is easily accessible, located on Iron Point Road, between the Prairie City and East Bidwell Exits from Route 50. We are excited to be a small business with a commitment to providing highly desired treatment options at very competitive prices. Come pamper yourself!

Why Choose Us

We are a no-nonsense business, committed to clear communication between client and provider. We offer non-committal and honest personal consultations,wherein we provide pricing up front, and offer evaluations we feel will be most helpful in achieving your wellness goals You are not required to buy anything if you come in for consultation; we understand that everyone is on a budget, and there is never a hard sell. We strive to make our services and products available to everyone interested.
