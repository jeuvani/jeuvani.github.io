About Us

About Jeuvani

After our first year of gaining many wonderful clients, we look forward to continuing to service clients living in Folsom, as well as others traveling to the area from out of town. Our director, Dr. Cynthia Cellucci, is committed to providing our clients with the best experience possible. We also have two excellent nurses named Jill and Bela and two amazing assistants, Jenn and Theresa, who are specialists in CoolSculpting, are knowledgeable about all components of Jeuvani, and will work with you from start to finish. We are fortunate to have Patti who is one of our nurses and travels to Gridley, California every 8 weeks to do laser hair removal, Botox, and Asclera on clients who prefer that location. Rounding out the Jeuvani staff is Tracy Helland who is our incredible operations manager. She is our "behind-the-scenes" gal who keeps us running like a well-oiled machine. Learn more about them below. We all look forward to working with you to meet your most desired goals.

Our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and community has been the same for many years, and is what continues to drive our business. Our spa is easily accessible, located on Iron Point Road, between the Prairie City and East Bidwell Exits from Route 50. We are excited to be a small business with a commitment to providing highly desired treatment options at very competitive prices. Come pamper yourself!

Why Choose Us

We are a no-nonsense business, committed to clear communication between client and provider. We offer non-committal and honest personal consultations, wherein we provide pricing up front, and offer evaluations we feel will be most helpful in achieving your wellness goals You are not required to buy anything if you come in for consultation; we understand that everyone is on a budget, and there is never a hard sell. We strive to make our services and products available to everyone interested.
