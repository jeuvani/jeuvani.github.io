Pearls of Jeuvani

Pearls of Jeuvani

Below are listed a few "Pearls" about our spa. Please be aware of these important reminders. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

  • Please always arrive with your treatment area freshly washed and "naked" ie: without application of lotions, deodorant, makeup, fragrance or anything besides clothing.
  • We recommend no sun exposure 2 weeks before and 2 weeks after each treatment. This is especially important for any of our laser treatments.  If the treated area must be exposed to the sun, please be certain to apply a reliable sunscreen or sun block.  We sell an excellent SPF50 sunblock made by Obagi.
  • We offer the following deal to all clients regarding every treatment we perform: if you desire a minimum of six treatments (laser hair removal, tattoo removal, CoolSculpt, any Icon treatment, any injectable, etc), and if you pay for five treatments up front, the sixth treatment is free.
  • We highly recommend no exercise on the day of treatment, but you may return to your normal exercise regime within 1-2 days. Most other normal activities can be resumed immediately after treatment.
  • After nearly all of our treatments, it is okay to immediately apply makeup over treated area the same day, and always okay the next day. Additionally, application of moisturizer and sun screen is always recommended after your treatment.
  • We do not treat pregnant or lactating women under any circumstance.
  • As you are arriving at our spa, you will be coming up Iron Point Road. After you pass McAdoo and Outcropping Way you will stay in the right lane and turn in where the number 1855 is painted on the white wall. Our spa is then located on the left.
  • Unfortunately we do not reliably answer our phones. Often we are not in the office and much of the time that we are, we are with clients. The best way to get in touch with us is to email or text us. We hope you understand.
