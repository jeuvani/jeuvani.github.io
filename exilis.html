More details about exilis

One of the most exciting innovations in cosmetic medicine, Exilis, has been featured on CNBC and Today News for its ability to contour the body, marginalize fat and tighten droopy skin. The Exilis machine has two heads: one which focuses on thinner crepey skin and another that focuses on fat reduction while tightening skin at the same time. This is an excellent follow-up treatment after CoolSculpt fat reduction.

The tip of the head becomes very warm and slides over your skin repeatedly for several minutes until the skin achieves the most desired temperature, frequently in excess of 100 degrees. We want you to feel the discomfort, but we don't want you to suffer, thus we adjust the machine settings as we treat for maximal results. We most commonly treat the lower face and neck for the tightening of the skin but we can also treat any body parts you desire, either for skin tightening or fat reduction in conjunction with skin tightening.

We recommend 3-4 stacked treatments, one approximately every 1-2 weeks for best results. No preparation is required for these treatments, except to withhold from application of makeup, lotions, or perfumes on the areas to be treated. Results typically are noted approximately 2-4 months after the final stacked treatment.
