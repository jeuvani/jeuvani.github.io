More details about fillers

Many cosmetic practices are sought after by people who desire increased fullness or volume to specific areas of their faces.  It is undeniable, as we age, we loose volume from areas we'd rather not!! Well, these fillers can instantly reverse that unwanted process. The areas most commonly treated are the cheekbones, the nasolabial folds, which are the ones between the nose and the corner of the mouth, the marionette lines, which are below the corners of the mouth, and of course, the lips.  We are skilled at making tiny but dramatic changes to these areas.

We inject slowly, having you follow each injection of only drops into the areas, so you are very much a part of the team regarding the injections and sculpting of your face. If you only want a small amount of volume added to a particular area, we are willing to have you share a syringe with a friend, with us applying a new sterile needle between clients, and thus reducing cost.  We will only do this with both parties giving consent.

Each of these fillers come with their own respective benefits depending on the facial sculpting you desire. Belotero is a smooth filler, resulting in gentle filling of skin that is more fragile. Juvederm is a smooth consistency gel comprised of cross-linked hyaluronic acid, one of the few acids that is FDA approved for use as a facial filler. It is excellent for hydrating skin and adding volume to applied areas. Radiesse, a calcium-based filler, works by boosting collagen into injected areas, and treating nasolabial folds. These three fillers all last approximately 9 months. Voluma is the heaviest of the fillers, is used to sculpt cheekbones, and lasts for up to two years. We recommend one syringe in each cheekbone for most desired result.
